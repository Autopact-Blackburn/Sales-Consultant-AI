How It Works

Commission Logic

Base Commission

Units sold × $100 base rate per unit.

$750 bonus unlocked at 18+ units.

$250 bonus for 23+ sign-ups in the month.

KPI Bonus Pool

Finance, aftercare, accessories, reviews and NPS all contribute. Unlocked progressively: 25% at 12 units, 75% at 15, 100% at 18.
